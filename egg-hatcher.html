Pokemon BDSP Automatic Egg Hatcher Overview


This project automates the egg hatching process in the Pokemon BDSP game by automating button presses on the Nintendo Switch Joycon. The implementation utilizes the Raspberry Pi Pico's GPIO and SPDT switches. The SPDT switch bridge points on the joycon to emulate a button switch; while the GPIO controls which buttons to turn on and off.

Background


In main series Pokemon games, breeding is essential for competitive battling. The process of breeding competitive Pokemon is to continuosly hatch eggs in hopes of getting the Pokemon with high IV's. These IV's impact the Pokemon's stat and how strong they will be. Competitive breeding is often tedious as you may need to swap the parent Pokemon when you get an offspring with a more preferred IV spread. Moreover, these eggs require the player to walk a certain number of steps in order to hatch. In Pokemon BDSP, the preferred egg hatching route is in Solaceon Town where you can ride your bycycle in a straight path up and down. This project emulates presses that go up and down this route while continuosly pressing ZL button to continue the egg hatching animation.
