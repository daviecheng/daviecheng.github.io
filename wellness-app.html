NutriHealth Overview


This is my design project for my Software Engineering course where I collaborated with 4 other team members. The objective was to build a wellness application that focuses on important features of health tracking. Features of the designed application include meal/exercise planning, weekly history tracking, recommendations, friends tracker, and a recipe marketplace. The meal and exercise plan is designed in a to-do list form where users can search for meals/exercises within our database to add to their plan.
